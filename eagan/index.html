Common Pest Problems in Eagan and How We Solve Them

Eagan, like many areas, experiences its fair share of common household pests. Understanding the signs of an infestation and knowing when to call for professional help is key to maintaining a healthy and comfortable living or working space. Here are some of the most frequent pest issues we encounter and how Apple Valley Pest Pros effectively addresses them.

Ant Infestations

Ants are persistent invaders, often trailing into homes in search of food and water. While many home remedies exist, they often only address the visible ants and fail to eliminate the entire colony. Different ant species require specific treatments. Pavement ants might nest under sidewalks and foundations, while carpenter ants can cause structural damage by tunneling through wood. Finding mounds of dirt near entry points, seeing trails of ants, or discovering sawdust-like material can indicate an ant problem. Our ant control services involve identifying the species, locating the colony, and applying targeted treatments to eliminate the entire population, preventing future incursions.

Rodent Problems

Mice and rats can be a serious threat to both property and health. They can chew through wires, insulation, and wood, causing costly damage. Rodents also carry diseases that can be transmitted to humans. Evidence of droppings, gnaw marks, scurrying sounds in walls or attics, and finding nesting materials are clear signs of a rodent infestation. Our rodent control strategies include trapping, baiting, and, most importantly, identifying and sealing entry points to prevent re-entry. We also offer recommendations for cleaning up after an infestation to remove health hazards.

Spider Concerns

While most spiders in Eagan are harmless, their presence can be unsettling. Some species, like the hobo spider or brown recluse (though less common in MN), can deliver medically significant bites. Seeing excessive cobwebs, finding spiders in corners and undisturbed areas, or encountering larger spiders can raise concerns. Our spider removal services involve identifying problematic species, removing existing spiders and webs, and applying barrier treatments to deter new spiders from entering your home. We also advise on reducing conditions that attract spiders, such as eliminating other insect populations which serve as their food source.

Cockroach Infestations

Cockroaches are notorious for their resilience and ability to spread quickly. They can contaminate food surfaces, trigger allergies and asthma, and are generally unwelcome guests. Seeing live cockroaches, finding their droppings (which resemble coffee grounds or black pepper), or noticing a musty odor are all indicators of a cockroach problem. Our cockroach control methods are thorough, often involving a combination of baits, dusts, and sprays to eliminate the current population and interrupt their breeding cycle. Sanitation plays a vital role in preventing future infestations, and we provide guidance on best practices.

Bed Bug Issues

Bed bugs are tiny, elusive pests that feed on human blood, causing itching and welts. They are notoriously difficult to eliminate and can spread rapidly. Finding small blood stains on bedding, seeing fecal spots (dark, rust-colored spots), or experiencing unexplained bug bites, particularly in a linear pattern, can indicate a bed bug infestation. Our bed bug extermination services are comprehensive and often involve a multi-faceted approach, which can include heat treatments, chemical applications, and thorough inspections to ensure all stages of the bed bug lifecycle are eradicated. This is a specialized service that requires professional expertise.

Wasp and Bee Nests

While bees are important pollinators, nests built too close to homes or in high-traffic areas can pose a stinging risk. Wasps and hornets can be particularly aggressive. Discovering a visible nest, noticing an increase in stinging insect activity around your property, or hearing buzzing sounds within walls can indicate a nesting problem. Our bee and wasp removal services are performed safely and effectively, removing the nest and taking precautions to prevent these stinging insects from returning. We emphasize safety during these delicate operations.

Mosquito Problems

Mosquitoes are more than just annoying biters; they can also transmit diseases like West Nile virus. Enjoying your outdoor space in Eagan can be significantly hampered by a mosquito population. Excessive mosquito bites, seeing large numbers of mosquitoes, or noticing them breeding in standing water are signs you could benefit from mosquito control. Our mosquito control solutions focus on reducing mosquito populations in your yard through targeted treatments and identifying and eliminating breeding grounds. We help you reclaim your outdoor living areas.

Wildlife Intrusions

Raccoons, squirrels, bats, and other wildlife can seek shelter and food within human structures, causing damage and creating health risks. Noises in the attic or walls, disturbed insulation, chewed wires, or finding animal droppings are signs of wildlife intrusion. Our wildlife control services involve the humane removal of animals, repairing entry points to prevent re-entry, and cleaning up the affected area. We address squirrel and raccoon removal specifically, among other common wildlife issues.
